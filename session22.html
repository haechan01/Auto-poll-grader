How was the Bayesian updating process used in today's class to determine the value of the imperfect information from the friend?

Ahmed Ibrahim

We had only prior knowledge about whether a company will go public or not. Then, we got imperfect information from our friend in the form of prediction, which has a probability to be true or false. The Bayesian updating process helped us calculating our posterior knowledge after this prediction, which is the probability of going public while knowing that the friend predicted public and going public while knowing that the friend predicted not public. Using these conditional probabilities, we could get complementary probabilities and calculate the expected value to make our updated decision.
